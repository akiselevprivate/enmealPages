Breakfast

Berry Banana Breakfast Smoothie

Ingredients
  • Broccoli 2 cups
  • Cauliflower 1 head
  • Coconut oil 1 tbsp
  • Brown rice 3 cups
  • Garlic 5 cloves
Preperation
  1. Remove cauliflower stem, pulse florets in food processor to make cauliflower rice.
  2. Heat 1T butter and 1T oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Heat butter and oil in skillet, add garlic and scallions, sauté.
  4. Add cauliflower, stir and spread.

Lunch

Chicken Fajita Stuffed Bell Pepper

Ingredients
  • Avocado 1 medium
  • Bell peppers 2
  • Grilled chicken breasts 2
  • Chili powder 1/4 tsps
  • Salsa 3 servings
Preperation
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix spices and quinoa together.
  3. Cut bell pepper in half and remove seeds.
  4. Fill with quinoa and chicken, then top with cheese.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes until pepper softens.
  6. Top with avocado and serve with lettuce and salsa.
